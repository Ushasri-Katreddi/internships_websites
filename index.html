ABOUT WORLD OF INTERNSHIPS

Internships are an opportunity to network with great people and sharpen your skills before entering the workforce. They also help tremendously with figuring out your true passion. Companies often look at them as a way to gain experience and exposure to make a smooth transition into your role when hired.
The number of hours an intern works generally depends on the time of year that they're interning. Fall and spring internships are typically part time and occur for the entire semester, which is four to five months. Summer internships may be 40 hours per week over the course of 10 or 12 weeks.
